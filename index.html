Every human breaksdown very badly sometimes when they need just a shoulder to cry on and be convinced that they too deserve love and care. But the question is can you give them that strength? CAN YOU KEEP HIM IN FRONT OF YOU AS HE DOES EVERYTIME? Well its upto you to decide but yeah we boys too need people to cry and throw all the things that's inside us. Every guy goes through a lot from their childhood to their teenage to their adulthood. But what do we get to hear when we try to express stuff, Clingy, Simp, Emotional Fool? Well that's just a few words that we get to hear! Change your mentality guys, Even boys may need to cry! You can't even imagine what we go through our whole lives! Just like we take care of you when you are at your lowest, give you care when you need it the most without thinking of ourselves, WE JUST NEED THE SAME WITHOUT JUDGEMENT!
